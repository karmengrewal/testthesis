While there is a lot of awareness being raised around pollution, marine pollution in particular gets overlooked. Marine pollution takes place when ocean water is contaminated, and takes a harmful effect on the water and life within.

Almost 80% of marine pollution is land based and due to careless human activity. The ocean is a big space however the damage starts with one plastic water bottle or one oil spill or one chemical reaction. Other causes are landfill spills, fertilizer contamination, industrial waste and others.

Another source not often talked about is non-point source pollution. The accumulation of small things that indirectly have an impact on the ocean as a whole. These things are septic tanks, cars, oil leaks, forests, farms, etc. It makes small and large bodies of water harmful to wildlife and humans.
